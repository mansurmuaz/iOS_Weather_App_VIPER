How to use Weather App?

Welcome to our brand new Weather App :)

  • First of all you need to give the location permission to the app in order display weather on your location.
  • After that you will see weather of your current location.
  • If you want, you can add bookmarks from the top-right "+" button. It will direct you to a Map view. Long press to the location that you want to add as bookmark, and then tap the "Save" button. It will save this location to your bookmarks and redirect you to the Home page. Now you will be able to see your bookmarks, search them by their names and delete by swiping to the left.
  • If you want to display the detailed weather information of your bookmarks, just tap on it. You will see the current weather of this location and the following 5 days' weather at the buttom.
